About

Fire and Test Australasia (FTA) is a 100% Indigenous owned and operated enterprise. We specialize in the data and communications sector, focusing on the design and deployment of essential data and communications systems, as well as fire suppression and management systems. FTA provides a variety of highly specialized fire detection and suppression solutions, suitable for projects of any scale - from expansive industrial setups to individual electrical cabinets.

Established in 2021, FTA capitalizes on our extensive experience in executing projects both in Australia and globally across a diverse array of industries. As a registered member of Supply Nation and a Certified member of Kinaway, we offer great value to organizations looking to engage with and support Indigenous businesses as part of their social procurement initiatives.
